What is Rumrejsen?

Definition of project

Rumrejsen is a group project that I worked on at Zealand. The project was about creating a website where we could sell trips to space. We also had to design/create pictures that would captivate the users.

My role in Rumrejsen

My role in Rumrejsen was to pitch our idea, and to pitch the idea I created these posters as a way to catch our target group's attention.
