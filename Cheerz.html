What is CheerZ?

CheerZ is a bar at Zealand Køge which is founded by me and some fellow students. I am the president of CheerZ and have been a crucial part of building the brand, creating events, and making Zealand Køge a better place to be.

My role in CheerZ

My role in CheerZ was undefined in the beginning, but as I was already the chairman of our DSR (Student Council) it was evident that I would also take on this responsibility in CheerZ. As a leader I had to stand in front and take the decisions for our brand, which has resulted CheerZ to grow a lot over a span of 2-3 months time.

Definition of project

In the start there was no party commitee at our school, so we had to build everything up from scratch. The objective was to create an experience for all students at our school and a great one.

The product

The product of this project is our 2 social medias platforms where we have showcased the work we have done. Feel free to take a look.
